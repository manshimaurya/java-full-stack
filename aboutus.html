PRESIDENCY UNIVERSITY

Presidency University, Bangalore, is a private university that was established in 2013 in Yelahanaka, Bangalore by the Presidency Group of Institutions (PGI). It is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and also a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). It is reaccredited with an “A+” Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The University offers UG, PG and PhD programmes across various streams, such as Engineering, Commerce, Management, Law, among others. It comprises seven constituent schools, which include the School of Engineering, School of Commerce, School of Law, School of Management, School of Design, School of Information Science and School of Media Studies.
